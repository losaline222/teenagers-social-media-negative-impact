Mental Health and Social Media

Common Mental Health Issues Associated with Social Media

  • Anxiety and Stress
  • Depression
  • Feelings of Isolation
  • Body Image Issues
  • FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)
  • Cyberbullying and Harassment

How Social Media Contributes to Mental Health Issues

Social media encourages constant comparison with others, often leading to negative self-perception. Additionally, the curated nature of content can create unrealistic expectations about life, success, and beauty, which can lead to feelings of inadequacy.

Moreover, spending excessive time on social media disrupts sleep patterns, leading to fatigue and increased stress. The rise of cyberbullying has also had a severe impact, particularly on younger users.
